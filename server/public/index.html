About Mousechat

Mousechat is a Student-Professor Interaction Software Interface. In short, it lets you ask the professor questions while still being as quiet as a mouse!

Choose to either host a room by clicking "Professor", or to join a pre-existing room setup by your professor by clicking "Student".
